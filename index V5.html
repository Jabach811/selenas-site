Her Story

From a library card
to a byline.

The short version of a long love affair with stories.

Raised on words.
Built to bounce back.

The daughter of a single mother who told her she could do anything, Cherry was reading aloud by age five — and quietly filling notebooks with poems and songs long before the world knew her name.

By day she holds her own in banking and finance in Charlotte, North Carolina. But not even a Fortune 500 could keep her from the page. Her stories blend real-life situations and hard choices with a down-home sense of humor — and above it all, her goal is simple: for God to get the glory.

— Cherry
